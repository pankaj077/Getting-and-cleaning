## activity label

* `WALKING` (value `1`): walking
* `WALKING_UPSTAIRS` (value `2`): walking up stairs
* `WALKING_DOWNSTAIRS` (value `3`): walking down stairs
* `SITTING` (value `4`): sitting
* `STANDING` (value `5`): standing
* `LAYING` (value `6`): laying down
